Sign Language Academy


Sign language is created for easy communication between a deaf person and non-deaf person & vice-versa. The app is designated to categories for different common and necessary communication phrases. If a phrase or word is not supported in the categories section of the app, is possible to access it through our web search option. The app also promotes the learning of the universal sign language through easily access lessons showing step-by-step hand gestures allowing for quick visual learning. The app has a simple design and is easily accessible. The app is easy access for users and can quickly learn to understand the different function of the app.
